Calculating the mean, minimum, and maximum of a column using Awk (vary `$3` to reflect desired column of data)

```bash
cat <input.txt> | awk '{if(min==""){min=max=$3}; if($3>max) {max=$3}; if($3< min) {min=$3}; total+=$3; count+=1} \
END {print "mean =", total/count, "\nminimum =", min, "\nmaximum =", max}'
```
